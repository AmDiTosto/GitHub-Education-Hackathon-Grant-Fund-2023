# FreyHacks
 - **June 24-26, 2022** 
 - **Smt. Sulochanadevi Singhania School**
 - **Digital/Global Hackathon**  

## Event Summary
FreyHacks was towards the start of summer break for most students, and focused on including begineer hacker and non-CS students.
